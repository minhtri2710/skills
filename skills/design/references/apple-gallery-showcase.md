# Apple Gallery Showcase

Use this pattern when the piece needs to communicate a large curated set of assets with calm cinematic motion.

## Good Fit

- product galleries
- keynote montage sequences
- premium case-study reels
- many visually similar assets that still need hierarchy

## Pattern

1. a stable stage
2. slow pan or drift
3. focused enlargement on the active card
4. depth via scale and blur, not chaos

## Avoid

- too many simultaneous highlights
- hard cuts between every item
- deep perspective distortion unless the concept needs it

## Why It Works

It communicates both scale and curation without turning into a collage.
