## How To Recommend Directions

For each direction, provide:

- a short label
- what feeling it creates
- what it is good at
- what the user trades off

Example:

```md
1. Information Architecture
   Strongest for clarity, editorial credibility, and structured data. Lowest risk, least theatrical.

2. Warm Technical Minimalism
   Strongest for thoughtful products and calm brand expression. Softer, more human, less aggressive.

3. Experimental Edge
   Strongest for novelty and attention. Highest risk, highest personality.
```

## Prompting Rule

Describe mood, material, and structure. Do not over-prescribe the literal layout unless precision is the point.
